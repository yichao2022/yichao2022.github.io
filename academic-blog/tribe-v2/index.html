Theory & Flow

TRIBE v2 (Temporal Reward & Intertemporal Behavioral Engine) integrates Discrete Choice Experiments (DCE) parameters into an fMRI-tuned latent space. We don't just predict choice; we model the neuro-computational cost of waiting.

\mathcal{P} = \text{Softmax}(\mathbf{W} \cdot \text{TRIBE}(\text{fMRI}, \text{DCE}))

1

Multimodal Input: Combines structural coefficients ($\alpha, \beta, \lambda$) with voxel-wise time-series.

2

Latent Representation: TRIBE maps behavioral friction to neuro-energetic signatures.

3

Policy Simulation: Real-time estimation of vaccine uptake under varying administrative "nudges".

Case: The "Neutral Trusters"

"Our findings in Wuhan reveal a segment with mid-tier trust scores. Unlike high-trust early adopters, Neutral Trusters are disproportionately sensitive to administrative friction. TRIBE v2 quantifies their 'waiting cost' as a function of intertemporal neuro-signatures."
